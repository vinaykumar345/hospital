# User Manual

This guide is expanded module by module for hospital staff and patients.

AI-generated content in the platform must be reviewed before clinical or administrative use.

## Signing In

Users can sign in with email/password or mobile OTP when enabled by the hospital. Accounts may require MFA. Password reset links and OTP codes expire and should never be shared.

## Hospital Selection

Patients and staff access the correct hospital workspace through the hospital domain or assigned tenant context. Branding may vary by hospital.

## Role-Based Menus

Menus and actions appear according to the signed-in user's role and permissions.
